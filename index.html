Why SeanBot?

SeanBot isn't just another generic moderation bot. It is a highly specialized toolkit designed for communities that need real reliability and power.

Whether you are running a complex multi-server network, scheduling cross-timezone events, or protecting your server from sophisticated raids, SeanBot gives you the granular control you need without the clutter. Tailor every module, customize your alerts, and keep your community running flawlessly.

Featured Modules

Anti-Raid Protection

Keep your server safe automatically. Detects sudden bursts of spam, quarantines bad actors, blocks phishing links, and lets you test your security settings safely with Canary Mode.

Server Federation

Connect multiple Discord servers together! Share live chat channels, sync forum threads, and maintain a shared ban list across your entire community network.

Event Reminders & Timezones

Never miss an event again. Schedule automatic alerts that mention the right roles at the right time. Users can save their local timezone so every reminder is perfectly synced globally.

Personal Reminders

Right-click any message to set a quick personal reminder. SeanBot will DM you when the time is up, and you can easily "snooze" the reminder for later directly from the message.

Event Signups & Team-Ups

Create beautiful signup boards for raids, game lobbies, or dungeons. Lock specific roles (like Healer or Tank), set group limits, and automatically generate private Voice Channels for the group when it's time to play.

Dynamic Voice Channels

Join a hub to instantly create your own private Voice Channel. You have full control to kick, ban, or lock your room. Channels automatically delete themselves when everyone leaves.

Secure Web Dashboard

Manage your server settings easily through a clean web interface. Secure token authentication keeps your configurations safe while you adjust your auto-watch alerts or game settings.

Server Logging & User Reports

Keep a clean record of server activity. Groups rapid events together to prevent spam, safely archives deleted messages, and provides a built-in UI for members to privately report bad behavior to your staff.

Community Highlights

Let your community shine. Pin the best messages automatically when they hit reaction milestones, create a server Starboard, or run live upvote/downvote tracking in your forum threads.

Reaction & Emoji Suite

Allow users to "subscribe" to messages via reactions. Manage your server's custom emojis, track usage leaderboards, auto-delete unused emotes, and right-click to react with animated emojis.

Returning Members

Never lose track of your veterans. If a member leaves and rejoins your server later, SeanBot will automatically remember them and give them "returning" role.

In-Chat Translator

Break language barriers instantly. Right-click any message to automatically detect the language and translate it into a clean, easy-to-read embed directly in the chat.

Fun & Utility Tools

Engage your members! Slap your friends (and track it on leaderboards!), ask the Magic 8-Ball, generate "fake quote" images, or instantly pull up random Cat Facts and GIFs.

Wizard101 Toolkit

Specialized tools for W101: Calculate complex damage buffs, instantly look up Spells, export custom deck images, track Beastmoon forms, and check live Treasure Card values.
