Why SeanBot?

SeanBot is a highly specialized toolkit designed for communities that need real reliability and power.

Whether you are running a multi-server network, scheduling cross-timezone events, or protecting your server from raids, SeanBot gives you granular control without clutter. Tailor every module and keep your community running flawlessly.

Featured Modules

Web Control Panel

Easily turn features on or off, set up channels, and customize SeanBot settings for your server right from your web browser.

Server Moderation

Easy moderation tools to kick, ban, timeout, and clear messages to keep your server friendly and clean.

Staff Tools & Ban Appeals

Give banned users a web form to submit ban appeals, save quick warning shortcuts, and set up community suggestion boards.

Member Reports

Allow members to privately report bad behavior or rule breaks directly to your server moderators.

Activity & Security Logs

Automatically track deleted messages, edited chat, role changes, and member joins in a private staff channel.

Staff Leave Requests (LOA)

Let moderators and staff request time off. SeanBot tracks start and end dates and notifies your team automatically.

Anti-Raid Protection

Keep your server safe automatically. Stop raid bots, block scam picture posts, and protect your members from spammers.

Raid & Event Polls

Organize raid groups with interactive role signups. If a team fills up, new members automatically go onto a waitlist.

Raid & Event Organizer

Schedule gaming events, set required wizard schools or gear roles, and let members sign up with one click.

Cross-Server Team-Ups

Connect with partner Discord servers so when someone posts a dungeon team-up, it shows up on partner servers too.

Community Calendar

Display an auto-updating monthly event calendar in chat so everyone can see upcoming events and tournament schedules.

Auto-Pin System

Let your community pin popular posts! Messages that get enough reaction emojis are automatically pinned in chat.

Activity Rewards

Track member activity in chat and voice channels to reward your most active members with special roles automatically.

Fancy Message Builder

Design beautiful announcement cards with custom colors, pictures, and titles directly inside Discord.

Emoji Manager

See which emojis get typed the most, lock special emojis to specific roles, and clean up unused emojis in your server.

Reaction Analytics

See popular reaction emojis in your server, react with animated custom emojis, and ping members who reacted to posts.

Auto Mention Replies

Set an automatic custom reply message sent whenever another member mentions you in chat.

Returning Member Roles

Automatically give a welcome back role to past members whenever they rejoin your server.

Beastmoon Tracker

Track Beastmoon form levels, check how many Lunari and Idols you need for your next upgrade, and view server leaderboards.

Wizard101 Pet Tome

Look up stats and hatching guides for over 1,000 Wizard101 pets to see hatch times, wow factors, and talent pools.

Roshambo Battle Royale

Host fun elemental battle royale games in chat where members duel using Fire, Ice, Storm, and Myth magic schools.

W101 Gear Lookup

Search Wizard101 gear items to inspect level requirements, school stats, set bonuses, and drop locations.

Level Scaling Calculator

Check what gear stats look like at lower wizard levels when scaling down for lower-level dungeons.

Damage Calculator

Calculate damage hits for your wizard by picking your blades, traps, aura, gear stats, and critical hits.

Deck Builder & Sharing

Create, save, and share card decks with your guild mates, generate deck pictures, and inspect share codes.

Spell Tome Database

Search any Wizard101 spell to see card picture art, pip costs, damage numbers, and extra card effects.
