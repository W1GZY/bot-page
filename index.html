Why SeanBot?

SeanBot is a highly specialized toolkit designed for communities that need real reliability and power.

Whether you are running a multi-server network, scheduling cross-timezone events, or protecting your server from raids, SeanBot gives you granular control without clutter. Tailor every module and keep your community running flawlessly.

Terms of Service & Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Terms of Service

By adding SeanBot to your Discord server or using our Web Dashboard, you agree to comply with Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines. SeanBot is provided "as-is" without warranties of any kind.

2. Data Collection & Privacy

We take user privacy seriously. SeanBot collects only minimal data necessary for bot functionality:

  • Server IDs & Channel IDs: Stored to maintain server configuration settings.
  • User IDs: Stored for moderation points, warning history, LOA status, and timezone settings.
  • Command Usage Logs: Processed temporarily for analytics and error diagnostics.

3. Data Security & Storage

All database connections utilize encrypted channels. We do not sell, rent, or trade any user or server data to third parties.

4. Contact & Support

If you have questions regarding these terms or wish to request data removal, please contact our staff team through the support ticket system or official Discord community server.
